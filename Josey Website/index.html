WE ARE SUNNNYS RENTALS

Locally owned and operated by native Idahoans Josey and Bryan Murdoch, Sunnys Rentals is here to help you
get the most out of your outdoor experience. We started small just renting out paddle boards to friends and
family, eventually expanding to jet skis, Polaris Razors, and many more outdoor recreation equipment. Our goal
is to help you have fun in the small vacation towns of Cascade and McCall Idaho.
